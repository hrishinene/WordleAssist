How to Use WordleAssist

A complete step-by-step guide to using the Wordle helper tool

Quick Start

WordleAssist is a free browser-based tool that helps you solve Wordle puzzles by suggesting optimal words based on your feedback. It runs entirely in your browser - no data is sent to any server.

Step 1 Open WordleAssist

Navigate to the WordleAssist web app in your browser. The dictionary will load automatically (you'll see "Dictionary loaded" message).

Step 2 Start Your First Guess

Click one of the word suggestion buttons:

  • Power word - Best starting word (defaults to SALET for first attempt)
  • Hard mode word - Word from remaining valid candidates
  • Soft mode word - Word using unused letters

Or type your own 5-letter word in the input field and click "Use".

Step 3 Enter Your Guess in Wordle

Copy the suggested word and enter it in your Wordle game (on NYTimes, Wordle.com, or any Wordle-like game).

Step 4 Provide Feedback in WordleAssist

After entering your guess in Wordle, you'll see color-coded feedback. Translate this to WordleAssist:

  • Gray/Black ‚Üí Click X (letter not in word)
  • Green ‚Üí Click C (letter in correct position)
  • Yellow ‚Üí Click I (letter in word but wrong position)

Do this for all 5 letters of your guess.

Step 5 Apply Feedback

Click the Apply feedback button. WordleAssist will update the remaining word list based on your feedback.

Step 6 Repeat Until Solved

Get your next suggestion, enter it in Wordle, provide feedback, and repeat until you solve the puzzle!

Understanding the Features

üéØ Hard Mode Word

Suggests words from the remaining valid candidates that satisfy all your previous feedback constraints. These words will always be valid guesses that match everything you've learned so far.

üî§ Soft Mode Word

Suggests words that use letters you haven't tried yet (or letters known to be present but in wrong positions). This helps you explore new letter combinations and gather more information. Soft mode words:

  • Use only unique letters (no duplicates)
  • Avoid letters already confirmed at correct positions
  • Avoid letters known to be absent
  • Can reuse letters that are present but in wrong positions (at different positions)

‚ö° Power Word

Finds the soft word with the highest power score. The power score (0-100%) indicates how effective a word is at eliminating possibilities, even in worst-case scenarios. Higher power = better word choice.

Note: For the first attempt, Power Word defaults to "SALET" (a known optimal starting word) to save calculation time.

üìä Power Score

Each suggested word displays a Power score. This shows the minimum percentage of words that would be eliminated even if you get the worst possible feedback. A higher power score means the word is more effective at narrowing down possibilities.

üîç Dictionary Lookup

Hover over any suggested word (the underlined text below the letter tiles) to see its definition in a tooltip.

Click on any suggested word to open it in Merriam-Webster dictionary in a new tab.

Tips & Best Practices

üí° Tip 1: Start with Power Word

For your first guess, always use "Power word" to get the optimal starting word (SALET). This word is specifically chosen for its high elimination power.

üí° Tip 2: Use Hard Mode for Narrowing Down

Once you have some feedback, "Hard mode word" is great for finding words that match all your constraints and help you zero in on the answer.

üí° Tip 3: Use Soft Mode to Explore

If you're stuck or want to try different letter combinations, "Soft mode word" helps you explore new letters while respecting what you already know.

üí° Tip 4: Check Word Meanings

Hover over words to see their definitions. This helps ensure you're guessing valid English words and can help you understand the suggestions better.

üí° Tip 5: Watch the Remaining Count

The "Remaining words" counter shows how many valid candidates are left. As it gets smaller, you're getting closer to the answer!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is WordleAssist free to use?

A: Yes! WordleAssist is completely free and open source.

Q: Does WordleAssist send my data to any server?

A: No! Everything runs in your browser. No data is sent to any server - it's completely private.

Q: Can I use WordleAssist on mobile?

A: Yes! WordleAssist is responsive and works on mobile devices, tablets, and desktops.

Q: What's the difference between Hard Mode and Soft Mode?

A: Hard Mode suggests words that match all your constraints. Soft Mode suggests words using unused letters to help you explore new possibilities.

Q: Why does Power Word take time to calculate?

A: Power Word calculates the elimination power for all soft word candidates. For the first attempt, it defaults to SALET to save time. Subsequent calculations may take a few seconds depending on how many candidates there are.

Q: Can I enter words not in the dictionary?

A: WordleAssist validates that manually entered words exist in the dictionary. If a word isn't found, you'll see an error message.

Q: What if I make a mistake entering feedback?

A: You can click "Get next guess" again to get a new suggestion without applying feedback. Or you can simply get a new suggestion and provide the correct feedback.
